# Federico Shop SEO Strategy

## Goal
Make `https://www.federicoshop.de` discoverable for brand searches and commercial-intent searches around electronic music records, vinyl, cassette, and CD.

## Technical SEO Already Shipped
- clean canonical URLs
- crawlable `robots.txt`
- live `sitemap.xml` with product URLs
- product structured data
- catalog and breadcrumb structured data
- social share images via Open Graph and Twitter image routes
- keyword-focused metadata for home, catalog, about, contact, and product pages

## Immediate Actions
1. Verify the site in Google Search Console with `GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION`.
2. Submit `https://www.federicoshop.de/sitemap.xml`.
3. Request indexing for:
   - `/`
   - `/catalog`
   - 10 to 20 important product URLs
4. Monitor Search Console coverage and enhancement reports weekly.

## Keyword Focus
- Federico Shop
- Federico Shop Berlin
- Berlin electronic music record shop
- techno vinyl Berlin
- EBM vinyl
- darkwave records
- post-punk vinyl
- electronic music cassette
- graded used vinyl online

## Content Strategy
1. Keep product titles clean: `Artist - Title`.
2. Keep product descriptions unique and specific to condition, pressing, and genre.
3. Publish fresh products consistently so sitemap updates remain active.
4. Build small collection clusters over time for genre keywords like `Techno`, `EBM`, and `Darkwave`.

## Visibility Strategy
1. Create and verify a Google Business Profile for Berlin local pickup visibility.
2. Get backlinks from:
   - Discogs seller profile
   - Instagram bio
   - Bandcamp / Linktree / artist-friendly directories
   - record community listings
3. Do not use fake traffic or indexing bots. Use Search Console, sitemap submission, and real content updates.

## Measurement
- Search Console impressions and indexed pages
- branded search clicks for `Federico Shop`
- catalog organic landing pages
- product-page organic impressions
